[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Midnight falls and the shadows play
I trace the lines where your secrets lay
Lost in the dark but I found my home
Safe in your shadow, I am not alone

[Verse 1]
The embers burn in the fading light
We pull the curtains and hide from sight
Your whispered breath on my collarbone
A quiet rhythm we call our own

[Pre-Chorus]
Closer now, I can feel the heat
Every mile of the world retreats
I drop my guard when you pull me near
There is nothing left to fear

[Chorus]
Hold me down in the wild unknown
Safe in your shadow, I claim my throne
Wrapped in the warmth of your midnight fire
Burning away in this sweet desire

[Post-Chorus]
Safe in your shadow, pulling me close
You are the magic that I need the most

[Verse 2]
I watch the moonlight across your face
A perfect painting I want to trace
The rush of blood and the steady hum
With every touch, I am coming undone

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Chorus]

Burning away in this sweet desire

[Post-Chorus 2]

[Bridge]
Time stands still when you look my way
There is nowhere else that I would rather stay
